Alarm Missions

Mission alarms can only be dismissed after completing the required mission. They are designed to help you wake up more reliably.

How to Dismiss a Mission Alarm

To dismiss a mission alarm, complete the assigned mission within the time limit.

You can change the time limit in the app’s Settings screen.

Mission Types

Read Your Phrase

Read aloud the phrase you set in order to dismiss the alarm.

Tap the speak button to start voice recognition.

Your voice is being recognized while the icon is moving.

Find the Dismiss Button

Find the correct dismiss button among several buttons on the screen.

  • Easy / Normal: Tap the correct button 3 times.
  • Hard: Tap the correct button 5 times.
Shake

Shake your phone the required number of times shown on the screen to dismiss the alarm.

You can adjust the shake sensitivity in the app’s Settings screen.

Yell

Keep the real-time sound level at 70 dB or higher for the set duration.

The current sound level is displayed on the screen. If it drops below the required level, you will need to maintain 70 dB or higher again.

This is the strongest mission for helping you wake up.

Watch the dB value on the screen and keep it above the required level.

When You Do Not Want to Use a Mission

Select “No Mission” if you do not want to use a mission.

Please Note

If you leave the mission screen without completing the mission, the alarm will ring again whenever the time limit expires.

If you are unable to complete the mission, you can restart your phone to clear the alarm.
